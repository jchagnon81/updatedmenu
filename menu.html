Appetizers

  • Causa Rellena $10

    • Features tuna or chicken, layered with silky smooth mashed potato and a vegetable layer with avocado. Topped with drizzles of sauces, and other vibrant garnishes.

    • A unique mix of shrimp and white fish marinated in pico de gallo, cucumbers, and lime juice. Served with house made tortilla chips, avocado, and limes.

    • Thinly sliced tender pieces of steak cooked alongside tomatoes, onions, and French fries. Served with rice and aji verde.
